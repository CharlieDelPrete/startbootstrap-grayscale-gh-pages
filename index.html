Kylian Mbappé

French superstar Kylian Mbappé decides to join Real Madrid after PSG contract expires.

Explore

Mbappé has been linked to a possible move to the Spanish giants over the last couple of years, but nothing ever unfolded as he remained in Paris. He will be joining Real Madrid According to ESPN, Mbappé has not informed PSG or Real Madrid of his decision but is expected to make an announcement next week.
